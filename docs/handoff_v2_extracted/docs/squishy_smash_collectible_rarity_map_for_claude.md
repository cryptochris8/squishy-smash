# Squishy Smash — Collectible Rarity Map for Claude Code

## Goal
Design the launch collection so players do **not** complete it in one sitting, while still feeling rewarded early.

Recommended launch target:
- **48 total collectibles**
- organized into **3 themed packs**
- **16 collectibles per pack**

This gives the game:
- strong early reward pacing
- meaningful mid-game chase
- hard-to-get late-game items
- room for future seasonal/event expansions

# 4. Progression Philosophy

## Early game
The player should unlock multiple collectibles quickly.

### Goal
Create excitement fast.

### Suggested early experience
During the first 10–15 minutes:
- several commons unlock
- maybe one rare
- progress bar fills visibly
- collection screen starts looking rewarding immediately

---

## Mid game
The pace slows.

### Goal
Create chase behavior.

### Mid-game experience
Players should begin:
- filling in missing rares
- targeting certain packs
- caring about combo performance
- caring about missions and repeat sessions

---

## Late game
Only a handful remain.

### Goal
Create prestige and long-term retention.

Late-game targets should mostly be:
- Epics
- Legendaries
- hidden/secret variants
- limited event items later

This is where “one more session” really matters.

---

# 5. Acquisition Rules by Rarity

## Common
### Intended experience
Unlocked frequently and quickly.

### How to obtain
- standard play
- early reveals
- first-session progression
- low combo thresholds

### Goal
Help players fall in love with collecting.

---

## Rare
### Intended experience
Regular but not constant.

### How to obtain
- normal play with moderate session count
- combo thresholds
- mission rewards
- repeat engagement in a given pack

### Goal
Create satisfying mid-term chase.

---

## Epic
### Intended experience
Special and exciting.

### How to obtain
- stronger combo performance
- deeper session count
- completing pack-specific goals
- boosted reveal events
- occasional reward boosts

### Goal
Feel memorable and worth showing off.

---

## Legendary
### Intended experience
Prestige.

### How to obtain
Should require some combination of:
- high cumulative play
- long-term pack progression
- larger reveal counts
- special event or boosted chance systems
- pity protection after significant play

### Goal
These should feel difficult, not impossible.

---

# 6. Recommended Unlock Conditions

Claude should not make everything purely random.

Use a hybrid system:
- probability
- progression gating
- milestone unlock logic
- pity protection

## Suggested rules

### Commons
- always available from start
- high reveal chance

### Rares
- unlocked after a small number of reveals or sessions
- moderate reveal chance

### Epics
- gated behind pack engagement
- improved by combos or mission completion

### Legendaries
- not realistically obtainable in first short play session
- require sustained play, progress, or major luck
- include pity protection so dedicated players eventually get them

---

# 7. Example Gating Framework

## Squishy Foods
- Commons available immediately
- Rares after 3+ food reveals or small combo milestones
- Epics after 10+ pack reveals or mission progress
- Legendary after deep pack engagement + pity support

## Goo & Fidgets
- Commons immediately available
- Rares unlocked with repeated burst play
- Epics unlocked through combo performance
- Legendary unlocked through cumulative goo interactions + pity

## Creepy-Cute Creatures
- Commons immediately available
- Rares unlocked after creature streak milestones
- Epics unlocked after completing creature-themed mini goals
- Legendary unlocked only late in pack mastery

# 11. What Claude Should Avoid

Claude should avoid:
- letting players complete most of the collection too quickly
- making legendary items impossible without spending
- making everything purely RNG with no pity logic
- making progression so slow that players lose excitement
- dumping too many collectibles into the game without themed organization

---

# 12. Final Direction

The collection should feel like this:

## First session
“Wow, I’m unlocking a lot already.”

## After several sessions
“I still have a lot left to chase.”

## After deeper play
“I really want that epic.”

## Long-term
“I’m still trying to get the legendary.”

That is the emotional curve you want.

---

# 13. Claude Directive

Implement the launch collection as:
- **48 total collectibles**
- **3 themed packs of 16**
- **8 common / 4 rare / 3 epic / 1 legendary per pack**

Then tune acquisition so:
- early play is generous
- mid-game creates chase
- late-game creates prestige

- dedicated players are protected by pity systems

This should become the foundation for retention, monetization, and future live-ops content.
