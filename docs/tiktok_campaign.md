# 07 — TikTok / Reels Launch Campaign

## Campaign Goal
Use short clips to drive curiosity, App Store taps, and a loop of social feedback into new content ideas.

## Content Pillars
### 1. Satisfying Burst Clips
Focus on the payoff.
Examples:
- giant pop moment
- messy wall splat
- perfect combo into burst

### 2. Weird Object Reactions
Show unexpected object behavior.
Examples:
- “I didn’t expect that one to explode like that”
- “This one leaves the craziest goo trail”

### 3. Meme Pack Teases
Do not lean on exact copyrighted names.
Instead tease vibe-based packs.
Examples:
- creepy-cute blind-box vibe pack
- cursed snack pack
- goo creature weekend drop

### 4. Behind-the-Scenes Build Clips
Show quick dev snippets, sound creation, object design, or particle tuning.
This is useful if you want founder-driven marketing.

## Best Hook Formats
- “This pop is way too satisfying”
- “I made the squishiest mobile game I could”
- “Watch this one burst at the wall”
- “This new pack is gross in the best way”
- “Why is this so satisfying?”

## Clip Structure
### 8–12 Second Format
0.0–0.7 sec — strongest visual hook
0.7–2.0 sec — quick escalation
2.0–6.0 sec — best destruction sequence
6.0–9.0 sec — second payoff or new object
9.0–12.0 sec — CTA or app name

## On-Screen Text Style
Keep text minimal and bold.
Examples:
- TOO SATISFYING
- NEW SQUISHY PACK
- THIS ONE POPS HARD
- WHY IS THIS SO GOOD

## VO Options
Use either:
- no VO, just satisfying audio
- very short ElevenLabs line
- founder voiceover talking over quick gameplay

## Posting Plan
### Week 1
- 2 satisfying clips per day
- 1 pack reveal clip every other day
- 1 behind-the-scenes clip every 2–3 days

### Week 2
Double down on the objects and sounds with the best watch time.

## CTA Examples
- Play Squishy Smash on iPhone
- New pack live now
- Which object should I add next?
- Comment your weirdest squishy idea

## Hashtag Direction
Use a mix of broad and specific tags.
Examples:
- #satisfying
- #oddlysatisfying
- #asmr
- #mobilegame
- #indiedev
- #gamedev
- #stressrelief
- #squishy
- #fidget
- #iosgames

Avoid stuffing too many unrelated trend tags.

## UGC Prompt Ideas
Ask viewers:
- Which object should explode next?
- Food pack or creepy-cute pack?
- More goo or more pops?

## Paid Boost Test
Boost the best organic clip first, not the prettiest clip.
Optimize for:
- hold rate
- replays
- comments
- profile taps

## Creative Testing Matrix
Test variations in:
- loud pop vs softer ASMR
- clean burst vs messy splat
- food object vs creature object
- no text vs big hook text
- satisfying mood vs chaotic brainrot energy

## Success Metric Priorities
1. watch time / retention
2. shares
3. comments
4. profile taps
5. App Store click-through
