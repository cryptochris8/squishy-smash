*Rewrite of `01_manuscript.md` against `book/ELEVATION_PLAN.md`. **Locked counts:** 21 featured (full hero treatment) + 27 gallery (page 31 thumbnail grid) = 48 total.

**Voice:** italic only when narrator speaks. Direct address ("you") encouraged. Periods, not em dashes. Mythic copy = fairy-tale paragraphs (no bullets).

**Per-character schema:** Name · Pack · Rarity stars · *Squishkeeper says…* · First spotted at · Signature squish · Pack-mate.
