Thanks for playing Squishy Smash. We read every message that comes in and we're a small team — please be patient if it takes a day or two to reply.

Before you write

Most issues can be diagnosed quickly with a peek at the in-app diagnostics screen:

  • Open the app
  • Tap Settings from the main menu
  • Scroll down and tap Diagnostics
  • If you see any errors, tap the copy icon in the top-right and paste the buffer into your message

That gives us the fastest path to a fix. Diagnostics include only error stack traces and timestamps — no personal information. See the privacy policy for details.

Common questions

Can I move my progress to a new phone?

Not yet. Squishy Smash currently saves locally only — there's no cloud sync. Reinstalling the app on a new device starts a fresh collection. Cloud save is on the roadmap.

A card I had unlocked is now locked again. Why?

It shouldn't be — once a card is unlocked under any path it stays unlocked, even if we tighten the thresholds in a later update. If you're seeing this, please send a diagnostics dump and the version number from Settings → About so we can investigate.

Can I delete my data?

Yes — uninstalling the app removes everything. We don't store any data on our end, so there's nothing else for us to delete on your behalf.
