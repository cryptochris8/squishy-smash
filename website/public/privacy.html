Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026

Squishy Smash is a single-player, offline-first iOS game. We are committed to keeping the player's experience private by design. This policy describes what data the app does — and does not — handle.

Short version: we do not collect any personally identifiable information. Game progress is saved on your device and never leaves it. Optional crash reports may be sent to a third-party service to help us fix bugs; these reports never include personal data.

01What we don't do

  • We do not require an account to play.
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any contact information through the app.
  • We do not track location.
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, microphone, or camera.
  • We do not share, sell, or rent any data to third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.
  • We do not serve in-game advertisements.
  • We do not use third-party analytics SDKs (no Firebase Analytics, no Amplitude, no Mixpanel).

02What is stored on your device

Your gameplay progress — coins, unlocked cards, achievement claims, settings, and similar state — is saved locally on the device using Apple's secure on-device storage (SharedPreferences / NSUserDefaults). This information stays on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party. Deleting the app removes it permanently.

03Optional crash reporting

To help us identify and fix bugs in production builds, the app may send anonymous crash and error reports through Sentry. When enabled, these reports include:

  • Error type, message, and stack trace
  • Device model, operating system version, and app version
  • A timestamp

Crash reports do not include any personal identifiers, account information, location data, or contents of the game state. Performance tracing and session replay features are not enabled. Sentry's own privacy and data- retention practices are documented at sentry.io/privacy.

04Apple App Tracking Transparency

Squishy Smash respects Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework. The app does not perform any cross-app tracking, so the iOS tracking-permission prompt does not appear.

05In-app purchases

The current release scaffolds Apple's StoreKit framework but does not enable any purchases for end users. If purchases are activated in a future release, all transactions will be processed by Apple; we will not receive your payment details (Apple shares only an anonymous transaction receipt and product identifier).

06Children's privacy

Squishy Smash is rated 4+ and is intended to be enjoyed by audiences of all ages, including children. The app does not collect personal information from any user, including children. There is no chat, no user-generated content, no friend system, and no external links from within the gameplay.

07Changes to this policy

If we change this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Material changes (for example, if we ever begin collecting any personal data) will be announced in the app's release notes on the App Store.

08Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach the developer through the support page.
